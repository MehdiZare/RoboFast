Welcome to RoboFast!

The ultimate skeleton for indie hackers to monetize plugins on the ChatGPT plugin market.

We leverage the power of FastAPI, Stripe, and Auth0 to provide you with a seamless experience.

Note: As of now, OpenAI does not permit integration with Stripe or management of subscriptions within the plugin. We hope this will change in the future to benefit the plugin market ecosystem.
